JUPITER
Jupiter, the grandest showstopper in our celestial neighborhood, is a colossal gas giant named after the king of the Roman gods. This vibrant giant, more than 11 times wider than Earth, flaunts stunning bands of clouds, including the famous Great Red Spot, a colossal storm larger than our entire planet. With an atmosphere filled with hydrogen and helium, Jupiter's swirling cloud patterns and colorful storms create an awe-inspiring sight. Jupiter is located over 588 million kilometers away from Earth on average.
